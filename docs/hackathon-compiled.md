# c0mpiled in Japan pt.3 — Hackathon Brief

> Source: https://luma.com/compiled-4qzo (scraped on 2026-07-04)
> This project (**Bonsai**) was built for this hackathon; the chosen theme is **Dynamic Software Interfaces** (see `yc-rfs-summer-2026.md`).

## Overview

- **Date**: July 5, 2026 (Sunday), 8:30 – 19:00 JST
- **Host**: Transpose Platform · Co-host: Ritsumeikan University · Supported by: OUVC
- **Format**: 5-hour build (starts at 12:00, deadline 17:00), teams of up to 4 people
- **Language**: presentations and submission materials must be in English (any language may be used for communication during development)
- **Special guest**: YC President & CEO Garry Tan in attendance (09:40 Talk + Q&A)

## Theme

Pick one of the three directions from YC RFS Summer 2026:

1. **Company Brain** — Tom Blomfield
2. **Dynamic Software Interfaces** — Ankit Gupta ← this project's theme
3. **Software for Agents** — Aaron Epstein

## Deliverables (submission materials)

- Problem statement & solution approach based on the RFS
- Overview of product, technology, and business model
- Demo or a demo video of **90 seconds or less** (judges review based on this)
- Market/user perspective premised on the global market
